# Story 17: Meme-Based Responses

Feature area: Future Integrations

Story:
As a moderator, I want meme-style responses, so that moderation feels lighter and more engaging.

Acceptance criteria:
- Responses comply with subreddit rules and policies.
- Meme responses are optional and moderator-controlled.

Feasibility rating: Low

Justification:
Image posting and content policy constraints are likely blockers.

Implementation notes:
- Use text-only templates or predefined phrases.

Modified story:
As a moderator, I want humorous text templates for warnings, so that responses feel less robotic without relying on images.
